About Me

Hi, I’m Vijay Kumar Dubey – a passionate Front-End Developer who turns ideas into interactive web experiences.

With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tools like React, Tailwind CSS, and SASS, I build responsive and visually engaging websites that are both user-friendly and developer-efficient. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and crafting interfaces that look great on every screen.
